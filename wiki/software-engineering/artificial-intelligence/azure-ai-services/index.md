**Azure AI Services**Â are cloud-based services that encapsulate AI capabilities. Rather than a single product, you should think of Azure AI Services as a set of individual services that you can use as building blocks to compose sophisticated, intelligent applications.

Azure AI services offer a wide range of prebuilt AI features across multiple categories and service (see overview below).

# Overview Azure AI Services
